Tiramisu

About the recipe

Tiramisu is a coffee-flavored dessert that features layers of homemade whipped cream, an egg yolk-enriched mascarpone filling, and coffee-soaked ladyfingers.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • white sugar
  • milk
  • heavy cream
  • vanilla extract
  • mascarpone cheese
  • brewed coffee
  • rum
  • ladyfinger cookies
  • unsweetened cocoa powder
